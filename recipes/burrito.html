Burrito recipe

Create the perfect burrito!

This recipe will help you understand the basics of making a delicious burrito.

We will lay down all the steps and ingredients on this page while making it as simple as possible.

Ingredients

  • Torilla
  • Beef
  • Beans
  • Salsa

Other popular additions

  • Cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Homemade guacamole
  • Rice
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Warm the wrap
  2. Add fillings to the centre of the wrap
  3. Add topings
  4. Fold in the two ends over the filling
  5. Fold in the top and bottom to completely close in the filligs
  6. Use foil to wrap the burrito tightly
