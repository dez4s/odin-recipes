Burger Recipe

Learn the art of making simple but delicious burgers!

In this article we will go through the ingredients and steps required to build your dream burger

Your friends will love this new skill of yours after just one juicy bite! Let's jump into it!

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Egg
  • Tomato
  • Sauce
  • Salad

Steps

  1. Wash your hands!
  2. Make the patty/burger
  3. Add salt and pepper on both sides and place them on a hot grill
  4. DO NOT SMOOSH DOWN THE BURGERS WHILE THEY ARE COOKING
  5. Cook the burger on one side, then flip it over and then cook the other side
  6. Toast your bun or smear butter on it and toast it on a pan
  7. Add the sauce, the burger and the ingredients to the bun depending how you like it and VOILA! It's that simple.
